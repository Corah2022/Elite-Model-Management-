Career Highlights

Participation in beauty contests: Miss Summer 2015, Miss Slovakia 2016.

Worked with known fashion / swimwear / bikini brands, such as Dolce & Gabbana, Vera Wang, Moschino, Blumarine, etc.


Physical Attributes & Social Media

Height: ~ 5 ft 11 in (≈ 180-181 cm) according to many sources.

Followers / Reach: She has several million followers on Instagram. The exact number varies depending on the source and when measured. Some say ~ 4-6 million.


Disputed / Unverified or Conflicting Information
